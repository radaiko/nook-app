Features

✏️

Markdown-First

WYSIWYG editor backed by plain markdown. Your notes are portable and future-proof.

☁️

Cloud Sync

Connect Google Drive or Dropbox. Access your notes across devices with version history.

🔗

Wiki-Links

Connect notes with [[wiki-links]]. Backlinks panel shows every reference at a glance.

✅

Task Lists

Track todos inside notes. The Todos view collects tasks from all notes with priorities.

🎨

Themes & Fonts

Light, Dark, and Nook themes. Choose your UI and editor fonts to match your style.

⌨️

Keyboard-Driven

Full keyboard shortcuts, slash commands, fuzzy search. Built for speed.
